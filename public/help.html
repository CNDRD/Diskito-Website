<amount> defaults to 1

,status <activity> [message]

Sets the bots status message
<activity>
"watching" / "playing" / "listening"
Defaults to "playing"
[message]is the message the bot will show
warning Requires Diagnostics role

,quote [quote]

[quote]
The quote itself / the quote_ID
If you send the quote_ID the quote will be deleted
Optional
You have 10 seconds to reply with the author of the quote
Optional
If you replied with an author you have 20 seconds to reply with the date of the quote (dd.mm.yyyy)
